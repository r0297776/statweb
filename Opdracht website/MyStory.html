My Story

I've gone through multiple phases in my life to get where I am now. Every work experience was in a different work field. Each work field participated in developing a different set of skills. I believe this is my most valuable asset. Adapting to all sort of situations and different kinds of people is what I do best.

-------"A brief history of my life"-------

Nowadays, I work as a first-line user support at the IT department of the Turnhout hospital. I combine this with the graduate course "programming" at Thomas More Hogeschool in Geel. My dream is to become a full stack web developer.

What i dit before? Before I started working in IT, I was a project manager. I was responsible for the development of interior projects. Based on the customer's needs, I designed their dream interior. The follow-up of the project was my responsibility. I took my measurements myself, attended site meetings and worked out full technical plans for every involved partie. Furthermore, all communication and administration were also part of my duties. It my job was to keep the various parties informed of everything that was discussedused. This continued until the project was handled in a correct and clear manner.
